About Us

Engineering Water Into Experience

Meet the Director

A Journey of Trust, Quality & Innovation

From the streets of small villages to proudly serving communities across Nepal, the journey of Supriya Water Technology has always been driven by one purpose — delivering pure, safe, and healthy water to every home.

Led by a vision of trust, quality, and innovation, the director of Supriya Water Technology has transformed a simple mission into a growing commitment toward cleaner living and healthier futures. Understanding the real challenges faced by families in accessing safe drinking water, the company was built with dedication, hard work, and a passion for making a meaningful difference.

Today, Supriya Water Technology stands as a trusted name in water purification, providing advanced and reliable solutions while staying deeply connected to its roots, values, and the people of Nepal.

With a strong focus on customer satisfaction, sustainability, and modern purification technology, the vision continues to grow — bringing purity, health, and trust from village homes to cities across the nation.

Mr. Sanjiv Kumar Chaudhary
