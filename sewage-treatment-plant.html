WHAT IS STP

Sewage Treatment Plant Solutions

A Sewage Treatment Plant (STP) is an engineered system designed to treat wastewater from residential, commercial, and industrial sources before discharge or reuse.

Our STP solutions use advanced biological and chemical processes to remove contaminants, ensuring treated water meets environmental standards and can be safely discharged or reused for non-potable purposes.

Eco-Friendly: Protects environment and groundwater from contamination

Treatment Process

1
Primary Treatment (Screening & Settling)
2
Secondary Treatment (Biological Process)
3
Tertiary Treatment (Disinfection)
4
Treated Water Discharge/Reuse
OUR EXPERTISE

Types of STP We Install

SBR (Sequencing Batch Reactor)

Compact design with fill-and-draw batch process. Ideal for medium-sized applications.

Apartments, Hotels

MBBR (Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor)

High-efficiency biological treatment with moving media. Best for large flows.

Hospitals, Industries

MBR (Membrane Bioreactor)

Advanced filtration with membrane technology. Highest quality treated water.

Premium Projects, Reuse

Extended Aeration

Low sludge production with long aeration. Reliable and cost-effective.

Residential, Commercial

FAB (Fluidized Aerobic Bioreactor)

Compact footprint with high treatment efficiency. Space-saving solution.

Limited Space Areas

Tailored solutions based on specific wastewater characteristics and site conditions.
