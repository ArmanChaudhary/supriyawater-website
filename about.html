THE STORY BEHIND

Why Supriya Water Exists

The Beginning

Sanjiv Kumar Chaudhary began his journey with Nokia and Hilltake, gaining expertise in sales, customer relations, and business development, which laid the foundation for his entrepreneurial vision.

The Turning Point

His daughter's illness from unsafe water became his inspiration. He traveled to India and China to explore affordable purification technologies, determined to make safe drinking water accessible to every Nepali household.

The Mission

"Pure water should be accessible to every family."

This vision became the foundation of Supriya Water Technology — built with one mission to serve the people of Nepal with affordable, reliable, and high-quality water solutions.
