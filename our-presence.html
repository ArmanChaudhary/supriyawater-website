Digital Lighting Projects

Digital Lighting Decoration

Illuminating Nepal's Grandest Moments

Digital Lighting Nepal, an initiative of Sakchhi Refrigeration & Solar Industry which is a sister company of Supriya Water Technology, brings innovative RGB pixel customized lighting to Nepal.

First Time in Nepal

NEA Headquarters Digital Lighting

Digital Lighting Decoration Nepal made history by installing the first-ever RGB pixel customized lighting decoration at Nepal Electricity Authority (NEA) Headquarters. This groundbreaking project received immense appreciation from the public and beloved former Managing Director Mr. Kulman Ghising.

RGB Pixel Technology
Customizable Content Display
First in Nepal

The project went viral on social media with thousands sharing photos and videos, creating an online public craze that captured the nation's imagination.

National Pride

South Asian Games 2019

Our team illuminated Dasarath Rangasala during the South Asian Games 2019 in Visit Nepal 2020 campaign. This prestigious project earned appreciation from the Ministry of Youth and Sports Nepal, showcasing our capability to handle national-level events.

Letter of Appreciation

Received official recognition from the Ministry of Youth and Sports for outstanding contribution to the South Asian Games.

Sacred Heritage

Pashupatinath Temple - Maha Shivaratri

For four consecutive years, we have had the sacred honor of illuminating Nepal's most revered Hindu temple during Maha Shivaratri. Our digital lighting decoration enhanced the spiritual atmosphere while respecting the sanctity of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

The project required meticulous planning, technical expertise, and deep cultural sensitivity. Our custom RGB pixel lighting created an ethereal ambiance that complemented the divine festivities, becoming an integral part of Nepal's largest Hindu festival celebration.
